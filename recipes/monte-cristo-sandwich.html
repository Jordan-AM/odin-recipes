Monte Cristo Sandwich

Description

The Monte Cristo Sandwich layers turkey, ham, and cheese between bread, dipped in egg batter, and fried to golden perfection. Sweet and savory, it's often dusted with powdered sugar and served with jam for a delicious twist on classic comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 teaspoon of mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon of prepared mustard
  • 2 slices of cooked ham
  • 2 slices of cooked turkey meta
  • 1 slices of Swiss cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Spread mayonnaise on one side of one bread slice. Spread mustard on one side of remaining bread slice
  3. Top with alternate slcies of ham, turkey and Swiss cheese
  4. Close sandwich with remaining bread slice, mayonnaise-side down.
  5. Beat egg and milk in a shallow bowl until well combined. Lightly grease a small skillet over medium heat.
  6. Dip sandwich into egg mixture to coat on both sides.
  7. Transfer sandwich to the hot skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides and cheese is melted
  8. Serve hot.
